The Best Movies According to Panos

Wings of Desire (1987)

A breathtaking cinematic masterpiece that weaves a poetic tale of love, humanity, and the longing for connection, set against the backdrop of a divided Berlin.

Groundhog Day (1993)

An iconic blend of comedy and philosophy, this film explores life's deeper meanings through a hilarious and endlessly repeatable journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Late Spring (1949)

A timeless masterpiece of Japanese cinema, this film beautifully captures the essence of Japanese aesthetics, tradition, and the poignant complexities of familial bonds.
